## Design Direction: Warm Boutique

Elegant but approachable. Warm tones from the logo. Clean white space, editorial photography feel.

| primary | #E8820C | #F09A30 | CTAs, booking buttons |

### Typography

- Headings: Inter or Playfair Display (elegant)
- Body: Inter (clean, readable)
